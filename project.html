Experience and Knowledge

The principals of United Construction & Development Inc .have developed a reputation over the past 20 plus years of striving for perfection. The firm has constructed new buildings, tenant improvements, and remodels. Some of the many notable construction efforts that the principals have been involved with in California include the following: Santa Clara Valley Water District Headquarters, potential “design and construction” award winning project on 43-acre campus; Story Road Plaza, consists of three new retail buildings and tenant improvements; Cornerstone Shopping Center, new building with tenant improvements; Blockbuster Video Center; Courtside Tennis Club, new fitness center; Los Gatos Swim and Racquet Club, Renovations; Crow Canyon Country Club, Remodel of Club House; Petaluma Office Building ,New 3 story Steel Frame Building; Kinko’s Copy Centers, Tenant Improvements, Ken Rodrigues & Associates, Tenant Improvement; Stanford University Laboratory, Renovation; Los Ranchos Steak House, Low Income Apartments.

All display an “accuracy” that is accomplished by delivering each project in a systematic step-by-step process to ensure that the design intent, both structurally and aesthetically, is fulfilled and exceeded. Building with such a methodical approach brings an assurance that every phase of the project is properly executed and therefore remains uncompromised over time. Ownership receives the best long-term value. This is what we strive for. This is our reputation.
