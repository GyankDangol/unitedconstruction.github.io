About US

Company and History

United Construction & Development company have been serving the California construction needs since 2004. Company has shared a commitment in providing the best long-term quality and value to our clients and project owners. It is our goal to construct each project in a methodical approach to ensure that the design intent is fulfilled and remains uncompromised over time. Our office and field personnel are highly experienced in construction and are known for finding and correcting flaws prior to construction. We work safely and rely on both innovation and structure to build our core.
